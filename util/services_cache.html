Services are a collection of buildings that allow access to artisan skill actions as well as your wardrobe and barbers to change your characters outfits and looks.

Levels

There are two tiers of artisan services.

  • Basic Service - Used for recipes with level requirements of less than 50 for the skill used.
  • Advanced Service - Used for recipes with level requirements of greater than 50 for the skill used.
