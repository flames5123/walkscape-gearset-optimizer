Recipes are ways to transform materials into refined versions (Logs and Bars), cook consumables, and create tools and items.

Recipes often have a service requirement in addition to the raw materials.

Recipes involve the Carpentry, Cooking, Crafting, Smithing, and Trinketry skills.

These skills can also benefit from a bonus of +1 Crafting Outcome per level beyond a Recipe's level requirement toward crafted items with no cap.
